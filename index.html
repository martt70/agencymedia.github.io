6 week duration - ending January 15th, 2019

Graphic Design and Online Marketing Solutions

The Client has hired Agency Media to assist Pro Business Office Solutions in providing graphic design and online marketing solutions in order to analyse and improve the company's business development plans and customer growth.

The following deliverables have been agreed:

  1. Redesign or improvement of the Business Logo.(See choices below)
  2. Design of Business Flyer for the Client.
  3. Design of Business Card with the Client's new Logo.
  4. Setup a domain hosting package of (500 MB) for 1 month.
  5. Set up a "Website in Construction Page".
  6. Set up email accounts with new domain name for one (1) person.
  7. Set up email shout out campaigns (using client's contacts).
  8. 15-second Company Video (photos and video frames to be provided by client.)
